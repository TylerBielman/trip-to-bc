Route Logic

Follow the coast to San Luis Obispo, Monterey, and Santa Cruz, then turn inland to Redding. Add one Southern Oregon overnight in the Jacksonville / Ashland area before continuing to Portland, Seattle, Port Angeles, and Victoria.

Before Each Coastal Leg

  1. Check Caltrans QuickMap and Highway 1 conditions.
  2. Download the day in Google Maps for weak-signal areas.
  3. Start with a full tank before Big Sur.
  4. Keep the inland US-101 fallback available.

Hotel / Ferry Call Script

  1. Confirm the selected room works for the dog.
  2. Ask for the total pet fee and any carrier or leash rules.
  3. Ask where the best overnight parking area is.
  4. For Port Angeles, confirm ferry timing and vehicle cutoff.
