Route Logic

Follow the coast to a two-night San Luis Obispo base, then continue through Big Sur to a two-night Monterey/Carmel reset. From Half Moon Bay, turn inland to I-5, overnight in Redding, push to Portland, reset in Seattle, then spend at least one night in Port Angeles before ferrying into Victoria.

Highway 1 + Ferry Watch

Recheck Caltrans shortly before departure and each coastal morning. For the final approach, reserve the Port Angeles-Victoria ferry once the date is locked and confirm dog/car rules before booking.

Before Each Coastal Leg

  1. Check Caltrans QuickMap and Highway 1 conditions.
  2. Download the day in Google Maps for weak-signal areas.
  3. Start with a full tank before Big Sur.
  4. Keep the inland US-101 fallback available.

Hotel / Ferry Call Script

  1. Confirm one 22 lb dog is accepted in the selected room or sailing.
  2. Ask for the total pet fee and any carrier/leash rules.
  3. Ask where the safest, most visible overnight parking is.
  4. For Port Angeles, confirm ferry timing, vehicle cutoff, and dog logistics.
