Hotel Call Script

Use this before booking anything that will hold the car overnight.

  1. Can you confirm you allow one 22 lb dog for these dates?
  2. What is the total pet fee, and are there room-type restrictions?
  3. Is parking gated, garage, monitored, or visible to the front desk?
  4. Have you had recent vehicle break-in issues on or near the property?
  5. Can we request a room that makes dog walks and luggage access easier?
