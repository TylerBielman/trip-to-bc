Route Logic

Follow the coast through Big Sur to Monterey and Half Moon Bay. From there, turn inland via CA-92 / I-280 / I-80 / I-505, joining I-5 before Redding.

Highway 1 Watch

Highway 1 is currently open through Big Sur. Recheck Caltrans shortly before departure and each coastal morning; slides or emergency work can change the plan.

Hotel Shortlist

Before Each Coastal Leg

  1. Check Caltrans QuickMap and Highway 1 conditions.
  2. Download the day in Google Maps for weak-signal areas.
  3. Start with a full tank before Big Sur.
  4. Keep the inland US-101 fallback available.

Hotel Call Script

  1. Confirm one 22 lb dog is accepted in the selected room.
  2. Ask for the total pet fee.
  3. Ask where the safest, most visible overnight parking is.
  4. Request easy dog-walk and luggage access.
