Route Logic

Stay locally in Lake Forest on July 28, then leave the Orange County area on July 29. Follow the coast to San Luis Obispo, Monterey, and Santa Cruz, then turn inland to Redding. Add one Southern Oregon overnight in the Jacksonville / Ashland area before continuing to Portland, Seattle, Port Townsend, the Port Angeles ferry, and Victoria.

Before Each Coastal Leg

  1. Check Caltrans QuickMap and Highway 1 conditions.
  2. Download the day in Google Maps for weak-signal areas.
  3. Start with a full tank before Big Sur.
  4. Keep the inland US-101 fallback available.

Hotel / Ferry Call Script

  1. Confirm the selected room works for the dog.
  2. Ask for the total pet fee and any carrier or leash rules.
  3. Ask where the best overnight parking area is.
  4. For hotels, ask whether parking is covered, gated, valet-only, or open surface lot.
  5. For Port Townsend, confirm the morning buffer to reach the Port Angeles ferry.
  6. For the Coho ferry, reserve the target Port Angeles departure and arrive at the terminal at least 60 minutes early.
